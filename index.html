About Sharon

A little bit about me: I've lived in Albuquerque for many years. Albuquerque is okay but one thing I like about it is the many places available to do outdoor activities. I love to be outdoors either hiking or walking my dog.

One of my passions is animal behavior. I love watching my dog learn and interact with her world. I find it fascinating how she is able to communicate with humans, dogs, and other animal species, and how she learns to solve problems. I've also just taken up bird watching and love watching how birds interact with each other and the world around them.
